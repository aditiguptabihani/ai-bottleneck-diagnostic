The claim

People who say they are blocked by AI knowledge gaps are often misidentifying the problem. The actual bottleneck is usually a specific learning behavior that prevents capability from compounding. A diagnostic that identifies that hidden behavior and predicts why their current fix won't work will trigger stronger recognition and action than simply reflecting back their stated problem.

Results that proves me right

At least 2 of 3 users report that the diagnosis identified a blocker different from the one they originally named, and at least 1 user takes a concrete action based on the diagnosis within 48 hours.

Results that means I built a horoscope

Users describe the diagnosis as accurate, but the diagnosis mostly repeats the problem they originally told me, and no user changes behavior or asks what to do next.

Kill number

If 0 out of 2 people take any action or ask an unprompted follow-up question after hearing their sentence, my hypothesis is wrong and my sentences are horoscopes.
